# Proposal: Meetup Copilot Launch

## Why
Boston AI Tinkerers is a meetup environment with lots of energy but limited time. People need help deciding which demos to see, who to talk to, and what to build when they arrive.

## What Changes
- Build an event landing page with sample agenda, demos, and attendee goals.
- Add an AI copilot that recommends who to meet and which demos to catch.
- Add an AI idea generator for hack/demo challenges inspired by the meetup.

## Capabilities
- New capability: `meetup-discovery`
- New capability: `ai-matching`
- New capability: `idea-generation`
